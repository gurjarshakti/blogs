Sir Chhotu Ram Institute of Engineering & Technology was established in 2002 with the aim of providing quality education in the field of Engineering and Technology. The Institute is a constituent part of the Ch. Charan Singh University, Meerut. The Institute started functioning with the admission of students in the B.Tech. courses in seven branches : Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Agricuture Engineering, and Computer Sciences , Information Technology,Chemical Engg, Electronics & Instrumentation. From the academic session 2004 one more B.Tech. branch Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering added to the academic programme of the Institute. In July 2005, a new brach Information Technology was introduced by fabricating the branch Computer Science & information technology.From academic session 2011-2012,the institute has also been entrusted to run M.C.A. programme which was being conducated in the University from 2004.
Engineering education & research is ever demanding field of study since the oldest civilization.industrial deveof nations was made possible by the movement of wheel,but search is on to improve the efficiency is wheel.We have reached from 8% to 40% and the search for still higher efficiency is more vigorously pursued today.The role of engineer is not confined to industries, service centers or workshops but has gone beyond.The half-life period of engineering education has been greatly reduced due to explosion of knowledge and computer database created by R & D.There is an urgent need to keep pace with the development by re-engineering with the course curriculum. Besides seven Engineering Departments Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication, Electronice & Instrumentation, Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering and Agriculture Engineering and a Division of Applied Science is also been here..

Vikram Chandra Goel
Message from Vice Chancellor

I feel extremely honoured to be the Vice Chancellor of Ch. Charan Singh University, Meerut (formerly, Meerut University). The University was established in 1966 to cater to the needs of higher education, especially in western Uttar Pradesh. It is one of the premier educational institutions of India. It has vast and beautiful campus in pollution free environment, which sprawls over 219 acres of land having vast playgrounds, experimental fields, botanical garden, rose garden, statues of several great personalities, gymnasium, indoor stadium, well equipped library, hostels for boys and girls, spacious auditorium, guest house, community centre, medical centre, weather station, etc. On the University campus, there are well maintained 20 regular departments of teaching and research in various faculties, like Agriculture, Art, Education and Science. Besides, 35 PG programmes of professional courses of various faculties, including Law, Commerce and above said faculties are also run under self finance scheme. An institute, named as SCRIET, is also located on the University campus, which impart education for conferring B.Tech. degree in various branches of Engineering and Technology. Further, to confer UG and PG degrees in medical sciences, university has a constituent medical college—LLRM Medical College, Meerut.

Further, there are 560  colleges/institutes affiliated to the University, which are imparting education for conferring undergraduate and postgraduate degrees, and diplomas in various disciplines of Agriculture, Art, Commerce, Education, Law, Medicine, and Science faculties. These colleges/institutes are spread over seven contiguous districts of western Uttar Pradesh. So, there are multifaceted challenges before the University administration, like maintaining standards of teaching and research, smooth and fair conduct of examinations and timely declaration of results, maintenance of yearly as well as semester wise academic calendar of the University and other developmental work. It is a matter of great pleasure that the University is already actively revising its curricula according to the latest developments in the respective areas of study.


It will be my sincere endeavor to provide suitable environment—with transparency, impartiality and fairness—to all teaching and non-teaching personnel as well as the students of the University so that all will work in responsible and coordinated manner with their full potential to make it possible to fulfill all challenges. I am sure that, working as a team, we would be able to take the University to the pinnacle of its glory.

  I hope that the years you will spend at SCRIET will not only be rewarding but most memorable.

 

Vikram Chandra Goel IPS (Retd.)
(Ex Director General of Police)

The Department of Computer Science Engineering is renowned for its cutting edge and for imparting state of the art education. We attract some of the brightest students and faculty.
